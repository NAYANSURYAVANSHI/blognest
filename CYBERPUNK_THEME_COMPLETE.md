## 🎬 Animations

### Gradient Shift
- **Duration**: 15s
- **Effect**: Smooth color transitions across background
- **Usage**: `.animated-gradient`

### Cyber Spinner
- **Duration**: 1s
- **Effect**: Rotating spinner with color transitions (cyan → purple → cyan)
- **Usage**: `.cyber-spinner`

### Pulse Glow
- **Duration**: 2s
- **Effect**: Pulsing shadow effect
- **Usage**: `.pulse-glow`

### Float
- **Duration**: 3s
- **Effect**: Gentle up/down floating motion
- **Usage**: `.float-animation`

### Glitch
- **Duration**: 0.3s
- **Effect**: Rapid position shifts for glitch effect
- **Usage**: `.glitch:hover`
