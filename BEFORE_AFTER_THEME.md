## 🎨 Color Transformation

### Before (Light Theme)
```

Cards: #ffffff (white)
Text: #111827 (black)
Primary: #2563eb (blue)
Borders: #e5e7eb (light gray)
```

### After (Cyberpunk Theme) ✨
```

Cards: rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.8) (dark with blur)
Text: #e2e8f0 (light gray)
Primary: #00d4ff (neon cyan)
Secondary: #b794f6 (neon purple)
Borders: rgba(0, 212, 255, 0.2) (cyan glow)
```
